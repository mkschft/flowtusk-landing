Cookie Policy

Last updated: 30 March 2026  ·  Effective date: 30 March 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how Makeshift Digital Oy ("we", "us") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on the Flowtusk website and platform. It should be read alongside our Privacy Policy.

We comply with the Finnish Act on Electronic Communications Services (Sähköisen viestinnän palvelulaki 917/2014) and the EU ePrivacy Directive (2002/58/EC), which require that non-essential cookies are placed only with your prior consent.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They allow the website to recognise your device on return visits and remember your preferences. Similar technologies include web beacons, pixels, local storage, and session storage.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

2.1 Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for the website and platform to function. They cannot be disabled. No consent is required for these under Finnish law.

Cookie Name Provider Purpose Duration
session_id Flowtusk Maintains your authenticated session Session
csrf_token Flowtusk Prevents cross-site request forgery attacks Session
cookie_consent Flowtusk Stores your cookie consent preferences 12 months

2.2 Functional Cookies

These cookies enable enhanced functionality such as remembering your preferences. They require your consent.

Cookie Name Provider Purpose Duration
ui_preferences Flowtusk Stores UI preferences (language, display settings) 6 months

2.3 Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with the website. All data is aggregated and anonymised where possible. They require your consent.

Cookie Name Provider Purpose Duration
ph_* PostHog Identifies users and tracks page views, events, and session data for product analytics 1 year
posthog_session PostHog Tracks current session activity Session

2.4 Marketing Cookies

These cookies may be set by our advertising partners to build a profile of your interests and show you relevant ads on other sites. They require your consent.

Cookie Name Provider Purpose Duration
__hssc, __hssrc, __hstc, hubspotutk HubSpot Tracks sessions and visitor identity for HubSpot analytics Up to 13 months

3. Your Consent and How to Manage Cookies

When you first visit our website, a cookie consent banner will appear. You can choose to accept all cookies, accept only essential cookies, or customise your preferences by category.

You can change or withdraw your consent at any time by:

  • Clicking the "Cookie Settings" link in the footer of our website
  • Adjusting your browser settings to block or delete cookies

Please note that disabling non-essential cookies will not affect the core functionality of the Flowtusk platform, but may affect the website experience.

Browser-Level Cookie Controls

All major browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. For guidance:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions

4. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are set by third parties when you interact with embedded content or use integrated services. These third parties have their own privacy and cookie policies which we encourage you to review:

  • PostHog Privacy Policy
  • AWS Privacy Policy
  • HubSpot Privacy Policy

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy periodically to reflect changes in the cookies we use or applicable law. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
